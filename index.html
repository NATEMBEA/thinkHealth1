About Us

Think Health Consultants Limited is a regional leader in health systems research, epidemiology, and evidence-based policy consulting. With a foundation built on over two decades of combined experience in public health, health systems strengthening, epidemiological innovation, and academic training.

We specialize in health policy analysis, systems design, implementation research, and strategic advisory services for governments, multilateral organizations, academic institutions, and NGOs across Africa and globally.

Our Vision

To be the premier African consultancy and research firm in health systems innovation and policy, catalysing equitable and sustainable health outcomes.

Our Mission

To generate, synthesize, and translate multidisciplinary research into impactful health policies and practices through collaboration, innovation, and capacity development.

Our Services

We provide comprehensive solutions to strengthen health systems through research, policy analysis, and capacity building. Our expertise spans multiple domains of public health and health systems management.

Health Systems Research & Policy Analysis

Comprehensive analysis and strategic planning for health systems strengthening.

  • Health sector strategic and operational planning
  • Health financing and budgeting systems
  • Universal Health Coverage (UHC) planning
  • Health system governance and leadership
  • Human Resources for Health systems

Epidemiology & Data Science

Advanced data analytics and disease surveillance for informed decision-making.

  • Disease surveillance and modelling
  • Social contact and transmission dynamics
  • Public health impact assessment
  • Big data analytics and digital epidemiology
  • Climate-health research integration

Program Design, Evaluation & M&E

Strategic program development and rigorous evaluation frameworks.

  • Strategic plan development and evaluation
  • Quantitative and qualitative impact assessments
  • Technical assistance to health ministries
  • Monitoring and evaluation systems design
  • Program implementation research

Training & Capacity Building

Developing human capital for sustainable health systems.

  • Leadership and management strengthening
  • Executive education in public health

  • Short courses and professional development

Technical Advisory & Policy Development

Evidence-based policy formulation and stakeholder engagement.
